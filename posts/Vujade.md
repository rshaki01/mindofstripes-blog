Vujade is the best thing to come out of fashion youtube. If 5 years ago, you were to tell me that a youtuber owned brand would be worn by Kendrick Lamar and have thousand dollar collaborations, I’d think you were crazy. Being one of the few English speaking YouTubers in Japan, Ken Ijima’s content offered viewers a glimpse into Japanese life and fashion. Through consistent, quality uploads, Ken garnered a following and influence one can only dream of. His first breakout item was his rendition of the Helmut Lang Bondage Cargos and his early collections focused more on everyday streetwear. Vujade NOW has become well within the scope of luxury fashion, introducing various denim, varsity jackets, hoodies made from recycled plastic bottles, trench coats, knitwear and more. What draws me to Vujade is Ken’s meticulous attention to details when it comes to his choice of fabrics, silhouettes, and his occasional integration of traditional Japanese techniques. Out of all the fashion content creators, Ken’s success has not only   been the most entertaining, it’s been the most inspiring to say the least.
